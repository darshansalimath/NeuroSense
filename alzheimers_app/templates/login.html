About Alzheimer’s

Alzheimer’s is a progressive neurological disorder causing memory loss, cognitive decline, and behavioral changes. It’s the leading cause of dementia worldwide.

  • Exercise: Regular physical activity boosts brain health.
  • Diet: Mediterranean diet with fruits, vegetables, and omega-3s.
  • Mental Stimulation: Engage in puzzles, reading, or learning.
  • Sleep: Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep nightly.

Diagnosis includes:
  • Cognitive Tests: Evaluate memory and problem-solving.
  • Imaging: MRI/EEG to detect brain changes.
  • Biomarkers: Test for amyloid/tau proteins.
